## Vulnerability Management and Patch Governance Readiness

The readiness binder includes vulnerability management and patch governance materials:

- `docs/readiness-binder/vulnerability-management-program.md`
- `docs/readiness-binder/patch-management-standard.md`
- `docs/readiness-binder/vulnerability-exception-register-template.md`

These materials are procurement-friendly readiness documents for healthcare vendor review and cybersecurity assessment. They do not guarantee remediation timelines, contractual commitments, SOC 2 certification, HITRUST certification, HIPAA certification, ISO 27001 certification, or other regulatory certification.
