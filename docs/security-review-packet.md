# Security Review Packet

This packet summarizes LumenAI enterprise security readiness materials for internal review, procurement support, and future readiness planning. It is an evidence-oriented index and does not claim SOC 2, HITRUST, ISO 27001, HIPAA, or other formal compliance status.

## Governance and Reporting

The readiness binder includes a governance and metrics framework for recurring security oversight:

- [Security Metrics Framework](readiness-binder/security-metrics-framework.md)
- [Security Governance Dashboard Template](readiness-binder/security-governance-dashboard-template.md)
- [Security Review Cadence](readiness-binder/security-review-cadence.md)
- [Security KPI Catalog](readiness-binder/security-kpi-catalog.md)

## Security Domains Covered

- Authentication and identity readiness.
- Tenant isolation and object-level authorization.
- Audit logging, audit integrity, and audit anchoring.
- Vulnerability management and patch governance.
- Rate limiting, abuse protection, and export throttling.
- Security validation, migration governance, and operational review.
- Incident response, business continuity, disaster recovery, and vendor risk readiness.

## Evidence Use

Security review materials should be paired with current test results, deployment-specific configuration evidence, reviewed management decisions, and open-risk tracking. Reports should remain factual and should not include secrets, credentials, private keys, raw JWTs, customer payloads, or unsupported compliance claims.

## Governance Disclaimer

These materials provide governance and reporting guidance only. Actual metrics, thresholds, and management decisions should be tailored to the production environment and organizational risk appetite.
