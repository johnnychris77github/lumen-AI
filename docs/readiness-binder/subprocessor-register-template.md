## Field Guidance

- Vendor name: Legal or commonly used vendor name.
- Service provided: Description of the service or dependency.
- Data categories: Types of data processed, stored, transmitted, or accessed.
- Business owner: Internal accountable owner.
- Risk classification: Critical, high, medium, or low.
- Review date: Most recent risk or contract review date.
- Status: Proposed, approved, conditionally approved, rejected, offboarding, or retired.
- Notes: Non-sensitive summary of residual risks, conditions, or next review needs.

## Handling Notes

Do not include vendor credentials, customer secrets, private keys, production infrastructure details, or confidential contract terms in this template.
