## Additional Readiness Checks

- Disable user accounts, service accounts, API tokens, webhooks, and integrations.
- Confirm data return, deletion, retention, or archival requirements.
- Review whether customer notification or contractual notice is required.
- Remove vendor access from monitoring, support, CI, storage, identity, or production systems where applicable.
- Preserve final risk review, offboarding approval, and data disposition evidence.

## Closure Notes

Document completion date, accountable owner, residual obligations, retained evidence location, and any follow-up review needs.
