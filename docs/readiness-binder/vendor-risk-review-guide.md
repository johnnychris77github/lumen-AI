# Vendor Risk Review Guide

This guide supports hospital procurement reviews, vendor risk assessments, enterprise security reviews, and subprocessor governance activities.

These materials are governance and readiness documents only and do not represent completed vendor assessments, contractual commitments, certifications, or regulatory determinations.

## Review Workflow

1. Confirm vendor service, business owner, intended use, and data categories.
2. Assign preliminary risk classification.
3. Complete due diligence and security review based on classification.
4. Review contractual, data protection, incident notification, and termination considerations.
5. Document residual risks, approval decision, monitoring expectations, and next review date.
6. Update the subprocessor register when the vendor processes or accesses customer-relevant data.

## External Sharing Reminder

Only share approved, factual, deployment-specific materials. Do not include vendor-sensitive information, credentials, secrets, private keys, confidential contract terms, or unsupported compliance claims.
