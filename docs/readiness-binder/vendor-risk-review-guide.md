# Vendor Risk Review Guide

This guide supports hospital procurement reviews, vendor risk assessments, enterprise security questionnaires, and security testing engagement planning.

These materials are readiness and planning documents only. They do not represent a completed penetration test, security assessment, certification, attestation, or regulatory determination.

## Security Review Workflow

1. Confirm the reviewer request, assessment scope, deadline, and evidence expectations.
2. Identify the relevant readiness documents and available control evidence.
3. Sanitize evidence for secrets, customer data, raw tokens, and private infrastructure details.
4. Clearly separate implemented controls, readiness materials, planned improvements, and residual gaps.
5. Route legal, privacy, contractual, and formal compliance questions to approved stakeholders.

## External Sharing Reminder

Only share approved, factual, deployment-specific materials. Do not claim formal compliance status or completed security testing unless those claims are supported by separately approved evidence.
