# Vendor Risk Review Guide

## Response Pack Reference

Use `docs/readiness-binder/security-questionnaire-responses.md` as the primary reusable response pack for hospital vendor risk reviews and enterprise cybersecurity questionnaires.

## Suggested Review Flow

1. Confirm the customer's questionnaire scope.
2. Select relevant sections from the response pack.
3. Attach evidence references from hardening PRs and repository documentation.
4. Review residual gaps transparently.
5. Avoid legal or compliance certification claims unless a completed third-party attestation exists and is approved for sharing.
