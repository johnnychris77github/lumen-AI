# Vendor Risk Review Guide

This guide helps prepare LumenAI security readiness evidence for hospital procurement, vendor risk reviews, and enterprise cybersecurity assessments. It is factual readiness material and does not claim SOC 2, HITRUST, ISO 27001, HIPAA, or other formal compliance status.

## Review Workflow

1. Confirm the requested review scope and customer questionnaire domains.
2. Gather relevant evidence from the readiness binder, security review packet, and security control evidence index where available.
3. Validate that evidence is current, factual, and free of secrets or customer-sensitive data.
4. Identify residual gaps and planned improvements clearly.
5. Route legal, privacy, contractual, and formal compliance questions to the appropriate stakeholders.

## Frequently Requested Evidence

- Authentication and authorization model.
- Tenant isolation and object authorization model.
- Audit logging, audit integrity, and audit anchoring design.
- Vulnerability management and patch governance process.
- Incident response, business continuity, and disaster recovery readiness.
- Security validation pipeline and migration governance evidence.
- Security metrics, governance review cadence, and remediation tracking.

## External Sharing Caution

Before sharing externally, replace placeholders with approved deployment-specific facts and remove internal-only notes. Do not include credentials, tokens, private keys, production infrastructure secrets, raw audit metadata, or unreviewed customer data.
