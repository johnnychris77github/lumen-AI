## Additional Readiness Checks

- Business purpose and service owner are documented.
- Data categories and access requirements are documented.
- Vendor classification is assigned.
- Due diligence evidence is stored in the approved repository.
- Access provisioning follows least privilege.
- Incident notification and support contacts are documented.
- Renewal or annual review date is scheduled.

## Approval Notes

Approval should document residual risks, compensating controls, required remediation, contractual dependencies, and next review date.
