## Breach Notification Readiness

LumenAI provides breach notification readiness materials to support healthcare customer notification review. These materials include PHI/data exposure assessment, tenant impact review, timeline tracking, evidence collection, legal/privacy review placeholders, and a customer communication template.

These materials do not constitute legal advice or a formal breach determination. Actual notification obligations must be reviewed with legal, privacy, and customer stakeholders.
